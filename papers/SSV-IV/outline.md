# SSV IV Outline

Working title: `Gravity as Update-Capacity Gradient`

## Scope line

This paper develops gravity in SSV as a mechanical consequence of local update-capacity gradients.

## Main thesis

Motion and interaction draw on the same local dynamical capacity of `Psi`; spatial gradients in available update capacity bend trajectories and appear as gravity.

## Core claims

1. Proper time is local change-rate.
2. Interactions consume part of the local update capacity.
3. Matter creates gradients in remaining update capacity.
4. These gradients act mechanically as pressure / refraction gradients.
5. Path bending, redshift, and attraction follow from the same mechanism.
