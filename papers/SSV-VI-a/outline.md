# SSV VI-a Outline

Working title: `Galactic Standing Waves and Flat Rotation Curves`

## Scope line

This paper studies flat rotation curves in SSV as a consequence of galactic standing-wave structure.

## Main thesis

Galactic discs are coherent standing-wave / soliton structures anchored to the central black-hole condensate, and this structure produces flat rotation curves without dark-matter halos.

## Core claims

1. Central black-hole scale sets a galactic standing-wave scale.
2. The disc supports a coherent solitonic structure.
3. The resulting radial profile yields flat rotation curves.
4. The model can be confronted directly with selected galaxy data.
