# SSV VI-b Outline

Working title: `Galactic Morphology as Overtone Structure`

## Scope line

This paper studies galactic morphology in SSV as the azimuthal overtone structure of the disc resonance.

## Main thesis

The 2D resonant structure of the galactic disc generates azimuthal overtone patterns that account for morphology, wiggles, and the contrast between smooth 1D rotation curves and richer 2D structure.

## Core claims

1. The disc must be treated as a 2D resonant structure.
2. Azimuthal modes organize morphology.
3. Observed wiggles and apparent smoothing are consistent with azimuthal averaging.
