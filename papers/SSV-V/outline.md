# SSV V Outline

Working title: `Condensate Black Holes`

## Scope line

This paper treats black holes in SSV as coherent condensate objects with frozen interiors and topological spin.

## Main thesis

Black holes are finite coherent condensates of the vacuum medium, not singularities; their horizons are acoustic freezing surfaces, their spin is topological, and information is stored in horizon structure rather than destroyed.

## Core claims

1. Collapse terminates in a condensate state rather than infinite density.
2. The horizon is a physical acoustic-freezing surface in SSV.
3. Frozen interior does not mean absolute spatial fixation.
4. Spin persists as winding, not as ordinary material rotation.
5. Horizon structure stores information.
