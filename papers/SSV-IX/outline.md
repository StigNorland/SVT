## Scope line

The CMB and the early-universe entropy budget are read as observational
consequences of the primordial phonon bath of the saturated condensate,
rather than as separately postulated quantities.

## Main thesis

The CMB thermal spectrum is acoustic noise integrated from the reconnection
events of the void-to-saturation transition (Stage 4–5 of Paper VIII), with
the present-day spectrum determined by the medium's equation of state and
the Kibble–Zurek defect statistics rather than by a separate radiation-era
postulate.
