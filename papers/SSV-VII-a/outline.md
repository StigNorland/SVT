# SSV VII-a Outline

Working title: `Quantum Mechanics from Hydrodynamics`

## Scope line

This paper derives the quantum-mechanical limit of SSV hydrodynamics.

## Main thesis

Quantum behavior is not a separate foundational axiom but the linearized / low-amplitude hydrodynamic behavior of the same medium, with measurement identified as an irreversible reconnection event.

## Core claims

1. The Schrodinger limit emerges from the hydrodynamic description.
2. Quantization is tied to circulation / topology rather than imposed separately.
3. Measurement is a physical reconnection process.
4. Uncertainty can be understood as a hydraulic bound.
