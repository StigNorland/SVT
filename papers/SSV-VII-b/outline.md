# SSV VII-b Outline

Working title: `Emergent Geometry and the Dissolution of Quantum Gravity`

## Scope line

This paper develops gravitational geometry in SSV as a macroscopic limit of the medium.

## Main thesis

If geometry is emergent from hydrodynamics, then the standard problem of quantizing gravity is mis-posed; the task is to derive metric behavior from the quantum medium rather than quantize the metric itself.

## Core claims

1. Geometry is derived, not fundamental.
2. Gravity and quantum behavior belong to one substrate.
3. Weak-field / post-Newtonian behavior can be treated as hydrodynamic limits.
4. The usual quantum-gravity problem is replaced by a derivation problem.
