# SSV VIII Outline

Working title: `Cosmogony from the Permissive Void`

## Scope line

This paper proposes a staged cosmogony for the emergence of the saturated vacuum in SSV.

## Main thesis

The present saturated, defect-bearing vacuum can be described as the end state of a staged growth process beginning from the permissive void, passing through perturbation, interference, saturation, and defect nucleation.

## Core claims

1. The void is treated as a pre-physical permissive condition rather than a medium.
2. A primordial perturbation is not forbidden.
3. Interference seeds structure.
4. Nonlinear growth leads to saturation.
5. Topological defects nucleate once the medium forms.
